Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the inspiration for Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY)?

The inspiration for SAGY is Mahatma Gandhi's vision of rural development and self-reliant villages.

Q2. What is the goal of Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY)?

The goal of SAGY is to develop model villages as examples of sustainable development and social harmony.

Q3. What are the values that Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) propagates?

SAGY propagates values like participatory democracy, transparency, and inclusiveness.

Q4. What are the main objectives of SAGY?

The objectives are holistic development of villages and improving villagers' quality of life.

Q5. What are the challenges related to local-level development?

Challenges include resource constraints, poor infrastructure, and social inequalities.

Q6. What should be the approach to achieve the SAGY objectives?

The approach should be participatory and focus on sustainable development.

Q7. What areas should SAGY activities focus upon?

SAGY focuses on health, education, sanitation, and infrastructure development.

Q8. What activities support personal development of villagers?

Personal development activities include skill training, vocational programs, and financial literacy.
